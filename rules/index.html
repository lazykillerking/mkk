COMPETITION POLICY

Rules & Guidelines

Read the following rules carefully before participating. All competitors are expected to adhere to these guidelines at all times. Violations may result in disqualification or a permanent ban.

01
ACCOUNT POLICY

Account Policy

  • Each user is allowed only one account.
  • Accounts are authenticated via Gmail.
  • Creating multiple accounts to gain unfair advantage is strictly prohibited.
02
SOLO PLAY

Solo Play Only

This is a solo CTF platform. No collaboration is allowed under any circumstances.

This includes:

  • Sharing hints
  • Discussing approaches
  • Helping others solve challenges
03
FAIR PLAY

Fair Play & Integrity

  • Flag sharing is strictly prohibited.
  • Any attempt to share or use another user's flag will result in immediate ban.
  • Each solve must be your own work.
04
ALLOWED TOOLS

Allowed Tools

You may use:

  • Search engines (OSINT)
  • Personal scripts and tools
  • AI tools such as ChatGPT

However, all work must still be individually performed.

05
PROHIBITED ACTIONS

Prohibited Actions

Do NOT:

  • Attack the platform or infrastructure
  • Exploit unintended vulnerabilities in the website
  • Interfere with other users or the system

Only interact with intended challenge environments.

06
FLAG FORMAT

Flag Format

All flags follow this format:

MKK{...}

Flags are case-sensitive unless stated otherwise.

07
BRUTE FORCE

Brute Force & Abuse

  • Excessive or automated flag submission attempts may be considered abuse.
  • The platform reserves the right to rate-limit or ban such behavior.
08
WRITE-UPS

Write-ups & Sharing

Users are allowed to create write-ups and share solutions. However, they should not:

  • Directly distribute flags
  • Ruin the experience for others
09
CONDUCT

User Conduct

  • Offensive usernames, abusive behavior, or misuse of support systems will not be tolerated.
  • Maintain respectful conduct at all times.
10
ADMIN AUTHORITY

Admin Authority

Administrators reserve the right to:

  • Ban users without prior notice
  • Reset progress or scores
  • Modify or remove challenges at any time
11
MONITORING

Monitoring & Security

  • User activity may be logged for security and fairness.
  • By using the platform, you agree to this monitoring.
12
FINAL NOTE

Final Note

> This platform is designed for learning and skill development. Respect the rules, play fair, and focus on improving your skills.
